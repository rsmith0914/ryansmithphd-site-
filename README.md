# Ryan Smith — PhD site

My personal academic site. Plain HTML/CSS/JS — no build step, no dependencies.

Ryan Smith — PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of British
Columbia, InfoVis Lab, supervised by Tamara Munzner. Research lives at the
intersection of visualization and health: building tools that help patients
communicate their medical story to their clinicians, in collaboration with
[Thrive Health](https://www.thrive.health/).

## What's in here

- `index.html`, `style.css`, `script.js` — the entire site
- `images/` — photos and SVG illustrations
- `cv.pdf`, `teaching-philosophy.pdf`, `smith2025-inputviz.pdf` — linked documents
- `404.html`, `.nojekyll` — GitHub Pages plumbing
